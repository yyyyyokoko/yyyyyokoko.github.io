When Data Science Meets Broadway

This data analysis project focus on analyzing the gross and economic impact of broadway shows. I worked in a group and build this project from scratch including collecting and clean multiple data sources, exploring associations among shows and reviews, and building predictive models to further discover relationships between sales and social media reactions. We also performed some NLP tasks like topic modeling and sentiment analysis to better understand the trends of shows. Here is a link to our website: When Data Science Meets Broadway
